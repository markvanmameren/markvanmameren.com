About me

My name is Mark, I'm 33 years old at the time of writing, and I live in the beautiful city centre of Utrecht with my girlfriend and cat. I would describe myself as a real teamplayer. I'm very motivated, entrepreneurial and precise. I have passion for front-end development, user experience and working hard on beautiful products!

Education

I graduated in Public Administration & Organisation at the VU Amsterdam and in Music & Technology at the HKU Utrecht. In 2016, I started my own company, together with 5 other co-founders. My main focus within that company was web-development, which I did for almost 8 years. This is also where I found my passion for front-end development, creating user-friendly and responsive websites. In addition to that creation process, I was also spending a lot of time on SEO and SEA optimalisations. In my spare time, I was working on mobile app development. I created multiple iOS apps that were published in the App Store. Those mainly helped people with music theory and musical hearing. I created those in Objective C using XCode and sold them using the Apple Developer Network.

Working experience

My fulltime IT career started at Sogeti. There, I first worked on an iOS and Android app for new employees in React Native and Typescript. After that, I worked full-time as an Angular Developer at the Dutch Chamber of Commerce. For the final product, a website that is designed to couple big corporates with small entrepreneurs, I built multiple components with their corresponding unit tests and SCSS styling. My current assignment is at UWV, where I work as an on a very innovative project, which has as its goal to merge over 200 UWV websites into a single Angular application. My job description is mainly building new components, unit testing and add NgRX functionality.
